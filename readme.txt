== Description ==

A sidebar widget that enables you to send any kind of mobile content to United States and Canadian cellular phone including ringtones, images, wallpaper and video.

**Features**<br />
-includes built-in widget<br />
-add your own header and bottom text
-enables any of your blog visitors to upload content from their computer to their own cellular phones

**Future Releases**<br />
*email me or visit promoteseo.com

== Installation ==

1. Download the plugin
2. Unzip mobilecs.zip
3. Upload the entire mobilecs folder to your plugins directory
4. Go to the Plugins page in your WordPress Administration area and click to 'Activate' mobilecs on WordPress.
5. Go to Appearance->widgets and add the mobilecs sidebar widget on WordPress widget to your sidebar

Please let me know any bugs, improvements, comments, suggestions.
